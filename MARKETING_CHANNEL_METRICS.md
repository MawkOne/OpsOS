- **Revenue per Email** ($ / emails sent)
- **Email Share/Forward Rate** (viral coefficient)

- **Device Open Rate** (mobile vs desktop)

### Optimization Focus
- Segment lists for targeted campaigns
- A/B test subject lines
- Optimize send times (industry: Tue-Thu, 10am-2pm)
- Maintain list hygiene (remove inactive >6 months)
- Target: 2-4 emails per week for engaged users

## üì¢ ADVERTISING (Paid Ads)

### Primary Metrics
| Metric | Good | Great | Excellent | Formula |
|--------|------|-------|-----------|---------|
| **Click-Through Rate (CTR)** | 1-2% | 2-5% | 5%+ | (Clicks / Impressions) √ó 100 |
| **Cost Per Click (CPC)** | Industry varies | <$2 (SaaS) | <$1 (SaaS) | Total Spend / Clicks |
| **Conversion Rate** | 2-5% | 5-10% | 10%+ | (Conversions / Clicks) √ó 100 |
| **Cost Per Acquisition (CPA)** | <$200 | <$100 | <$50 | Total Spend / Conversions |
| **Return on Ad Spend (ROAS)** | 2:1 | 4:1 | 8:1+ | Revenue / Ad Spend |
| **Quality Score** | 5-7 | 7-9 | 9-10 | Google Ads metric (1-10) |
| **Impression Share** | 50-70% | 70-90% | 90%+ | Your Impressions / Total Available |

### Secondary Metrics
- **Cost Per Mille (CPM)** (cost per 1,000 impressions)
- **View-Through Conversions** (saw ad, converted later)
- **Ad Frequency** (times shown to same user)
- **Landing Page Experience Score**
- **Customer Lifetime Value (CLV) / CPA Ratio**

### Optimization Focus
- Maintain Quality Score 8+ (lowers CPC by 50%)
- Test 3-5 ad variations per campaign
- Use negative keywords aggressively
- Target CPA <20% of customer LTV
- Optimize landing pages for >40% conversion rate
- Retarget website visitors (2-5x better ROAS)

## üìÑ PAGES (Website/Landing Pages)

### Primary Metrics
| Metric | Good | Great | Excellent | Formula |
|--------|------|-------|-----------|---------|
| **Conversion Rate** | 2-5% | 5-10% | 10%+ | (Conversions / Visits) √ó 100 |
| **Bounce Rate** | 40-60% | 30-40% | <30% | Left Without Action / Total Visits |
| **Time on Page** | 30-60 sec | 1-2 min | 2+ min | Average session duration |
| **Page Load Time** | 2-4 sec | 1-2 sec | <1 sec | Time to Interactive |
| **Scroll Depth** | 50-70% | 70-85% | 85%+ | % of Page Scrolled |
| **Form Completion Rate** | 20-40% | 40-60% | 60%+ | Submitted / Started |
| **Exit Rate** | <40% | <30% | <20% | Exited on Page / Total Views |

### Page-Specific Benchmarks
- **Homepage**: <30% bounce rate
- **Product Pages**: 40-60% bounce rate
- **Landing Pages**: 70-90% bounce rate (acceptable - single purpose)
- **Blog Posts**: 70-80% bounce rate (acceptable - informational)
- **Pricing Pages**: 20-30% bounce rate (high intent)

### Secondary Metrics
- **Above-the-Fold Content Visibility**
- **CTA Click Rate** (clicks on call-to-action)
- **Mobile vs Desktop Performance**
- **Return Visitor Rate**
- **Page Value** ($ per pageview)

### Optimization Focus
- Clear value proposition in first 3 seconds
- Single, prominent CTA above the fold
- Reduce form fields (each field = 5-10% drop-off)
- Mobile-first design (>60% traffic mobile)
- Page speed <3 seconds (every 1 sec = 7% conversion loss)
- A/B test headlines, CTAs, forms continuously

|--------|------|-------|-----------|---------|

### Content Performance Tiers
- **Pillar Content** (2,000-4,000 words): 500-2,000 visitors/mo
- **Standard Posts** (800-1,500 words): 100-500 visitors/mo
- **Quick Posts** (<800 words): 50-200 visitors/mo

### Secondary Metrics
- **Content Decay Rate** (traffic drop over time)
- **Internal Link Clicks** (navigation to other content)
- **Comment Rate** (comments / views)
- **Email Capture Rate** (subscribers / readers)
- **Featured Snippet Wins** (% of target keywords)
- **Pages per Session from Article**

### Optimization Focus
- Publish 2-4 articles per week minimum
- Target 1,500-2,500 words for SEO
- Include original data/research (gets 3x backlinks)
- Use visuals every 300 words
- Update top 20% articles quarterly
- Build topic clusters (hub & spoke model)
- Target "how to" and "best" keywords
- Include downloadable resources (lead magnets)

## üí° KEY INSIGHTS

### Volume vs Quality Balance
- **High Volume**: Email, SEO, Articles (compound over time)
- **High Quality**: Paid Ads, Landing Pages (immediate ROI)
- **Community Building**: Social, Articles (long-term brand)

### Investment Priority (Early Stage)
1. **SEO + Content** (6-12 months to payoff, but highest ROI long-term)
2. **Paid Ads** (immediate feedback, validate messaging)
3. **Email** (highest conversion rate, nurture leads)
4. **Landing Pages** (multiply effectiveness of all channels)
5. **Social** (brand building, community engagement)

### Red Flags to Watch
- **Email**: Open rate <10% (list quality issue)
- **SEO**: Bounce rate >80% (content/keyword mismatch)
- **Paid Ads**: CPA >50% of LTV (unprofitable)
- **Social**: Engagement <0.5% (content not resonating)
- **Pages**: Bounce rate >70% (except blogs)
- **Articles**: Time on page <1 min (poor content quality)
