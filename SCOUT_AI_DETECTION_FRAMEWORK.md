# Scout AI Detection Framework
**3-Layer Marketing Intelligence System**

## Overview

Scout AI operates on **3 detection layers** to catch issues, spot trends, and identify strategic opportunities across all marketing channels:

1. **‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)** - Catch breaking issues before they become expensive
2. **üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)** - Spot emerging patterns before humans notice
3. **üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)** - Understand long-term impact and structural gaps

---

## üìß EMAIL

### ‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)
1. **Deliverability crash** - Bounce rate >10% (normal <2%)
   - Alert: "Email deliverability issue - check sender reputation"
   
2. **Send failure** - Scheduled email didn't send
   - Alert: "Campaign X scheduled for today - no sends recorded"
   
3. **Unsubscribe spike** - Unsub rate >5% (normal <0.5%)
   - Alert: "Campaign Y causing mass unsubscribes - pause and review"
   
4. **Open rate crash** - <10% (normal 20-40%)
   - Alert: "Subject line or timing issue - opens down 70%"

### üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)
1. **Engagement decay** - Open rate declining 3+ weeks
   - Alert: "Email engagement down 15% over 3 weeks - list fatigue or content issues"
   
2. **List growth stagnation** - No new subscribers 2+ weeks
   - Alert: "Email list growth stopped - check opt-in forms"
   
3. **Click-through declining** - CTR down week-over-week
   - Alert: "Email clicks declining but opens stable - content relevance issue"
   
4. **Segment performance shift** - One segment suddenly performing better
   - Alert: "Segment X responding 30% better - scale this approach"

### üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. **Volume gap** - Sending 1/month vs. 8-12 benchmark
   - Alert: "Major email underutilization - missing 20-30% of potential revenue"
   
2. **Automation gap** - No welcome series, no nurture flows
   - Alert: "No automated email flows - losing 70% of lead value"
   
3. **Revenue attribution** - Email driving X% of revenue vs. Y% benchmark
   - Alert: "Email drives 5% of revenue vs. 25% industry avg - underdeveloped channel"
   
4. **List health** - Inactive subscribers >50%
   - Alert: "Half your list is dead - hurting deliverability and costing money"

## üì± SOCIAL

### ‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)
1. **Viral spike** - Post getting 10x normal engagement
   - Alert: "Post X going viral - capitalize now with paid boost"
   
2. **Negative sentiment surge** - Comments/mentions turning negative
   - Alert: "Brand crisis detected - negative sentiment up 500%"
   
3. **Follower drop** - Lost 5%+ followers in 24 hours
   - Alert: "Mass unfollows - controversial post or bot purge?"
   
4. **Engagement crash** - Reach down 70%+ suddenly
   - Alert: "Algorithm change or platform issue affecting reach"

### üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)
1. **Content format winner** - Video posts outperforming 3+ weeks
   - Alert: "Video content getting 3x engagement - shift content mix"
   
2. **Posting frequency impact** - More posts = declining engagement
   - Alert: "Over-posting causing fatigue - reduce frequency"
   
3. **Audience growth velocity** - Follower growth accelerating/decelerating
   - Alert: "Growth slowing 20% week-over-week - need content refresh"
   
4. **Best time shifting** - Optimal posting time changing
   - Alert: "Audience activity shifted to mornings - adjust schedule"

### üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. **Channel presence gap** - Active on 1 platform vs. 3-4 benchmark
   - Alert: "Only on Twitter - missing Instagram/LinkedIn audience"
   
2. **Organic vs. paid mix** - 100% organic or 100% paid
   - Alert: "No organic social - over-reliant on paid reach"
   
3. **Community engagement** - Posting but not responding
   - Alert: "Zero replies/DMs - missing community-building opportunity"
   
4. **Content-to-conversion gap** - High engagement, low traffic
   - Alert: "100K impressions but 50 clicks - add CTAs and links"

## üîç SEO

### ‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)
1. **Ranking collapse** - Top 3 keyword drops to page 2+
   - Alert: "Primary keyword lost 20 positions overnight - technical issue?"
   
2. **Organic traffic cliff** - 50%+ drop in 24 hours
   - Alert: "Algorithm update or site penalty - urgent investigation needed"
   
3. **Indexing issue** - Pages suddenly de-indexed
   - Alert: "30 pages removed from Google index - check robots.txt/penalties"
   
4. **Core Web Vitals failure** - Site speed/performance crash
   - Alert: "Page load time doubled - users bouncing, rankings at risk"

### üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)
1. **Striking distance keywords** - Ranking 11-20, showing upward momentum
   - Alert: "5 keywords trending toward page 1 - optimization opportunity"
   
2. **Rank decay** - Slow slide from position 3 ‚Üí 5 ‚Üí 8
   - Alert: "Keyword X declining steadily - competitors improving or content aging"
   
3. **SERP feature loss** - Lost featured snippet/people also ask
   - Alert: "Featured snippet stolen by competitor - content update needed"
   
4. **Click-through rate decline** - Rankings stable but traffic down
   - Alert: "CTR declining - meta descriptions/titles losing relevance"

### üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. **Content gap analysis** - Missing content for high-volume keywords
   - Alert: "500 high-value keywords with no content - 6-month opportunity map"
   
2. **Technical debt** - 40% of pages have SEO issues
   - Alert: "Widespread technical SEO problems limiting all rankings"
   
3. **Backlink profile stagnation** - No new quality backlinks in 6 months
   - Alert: "Link building program needed - competitors gaining authority"
   
4. **Keyword cannibalization** - Multiple pages competing for same terms
   - Alert: "50 instances of self-competition - consolidation opportunity"

## ‚úçÔ∏è CONTENT

### ‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)
1. **Page error spike** - 404s or 500 errors on published content
   - Alert: "5 blog posts returning errors - fix immediately"
   
2. **New content underperforming** - Published but zero traffic
   - Alert: "Yesterday's post has zero views - promotion or indexing issue?"
   
3. **Viral content opportunity** - Old post suddenly getting traffic
   - Alert: "2-year-old post spiking - update and capitalize"
   
4. **Negative user signals** - High bounce rate on new content
   - Alert: "90% bounce rate on new article - content/UX issue"

### üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)
1. **Topic momentum** - Certain topics driving increasing traffic
   - Alert: "AI content generating 40% more traffic each week - double down"
   
2. **Content format winner** - Long-form outperforming short-form
   - Alert: "2,000+ word posts get 5x traffic - shift content strategy"
   
3. **Conversion rate by topic** - Some topics convert better
   - Alert: "Tutorial content converts 3x better than news - prioritize"
   
4. **Content decay rate** - Posts losing traffic faster than usual
   - Alert: "Content aging 2x faster than industry avg - freshness strategy needed"

### üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. **Publishing volume gap** - 1 post/month vs. 8-12 benchmark
   - Alert: "Massive content gap - competitors publishing 10x more"
   
2. **Content-to-lead ratio** - 100 posts but only 50 leads/month
   - Alert: "Content driving traffic but not leads - CTA/offer problem"
   
3. **Pillar content gap** - No comprehensive guides or resources
   - Alert: "No evergreen pillar content - missing authority-building opportunity"
   
4. **Content ROI analysis** - Which content types drive revenue
   - Alert: "Case studies drive 10x ROI vs. blog posts - reallocate effort"

## üí∞ ADVERTISING (PAID)

### ‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)
1. **Budget burn without conversions** - Spent $500, 0 conversions
   - Alert: "Campaign X burning budget with no results - pause now"
   
2. **CPA spike** - Cost per acquisition doubled overnight
   - Alert: "Campaign Y CPA went from $50 ‚Üí $120 - creative fatigue or audience saturation"
   
3. **Ad disapproval** - Campaign rejected by platform
   - Alert: "Ad Z disapproved - campaign paused, check policy violations"
   
4. **Click fraud spike** - High clicks, zero engagement
   - Alert: "Bot traffic detected on Campaign A - pause and investigate"

### üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)
1. **Creative fatigue** - CTR declining over 2-3 weeks
   - Alert: "Ad creative performance down 30% - time to refresh"
   
2. **Audience scaling impact** - CPA rising as spend increases
   - Alert: "Audience saturation - CPA up 40% as budget scaled"
   
3. **Competitive pressure** - CPM rising week-over-week
   - Alert: "Auction getting more expensive - competitors increasing spend"
   
4. **Winning creative pattern** - Video ads outperforming 3+ weeks
   - Alert: "Video creative getting 3x ROAS vs. static - shift budget"

### üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. **Channel diversification** - 100% budget on one platform
   - Alert: "All spend on Google - missing Meta/LinkedIn/TikTok audience"
   
2. **Retargeting gap** - No retargeting campaigns active
   - Alert: "Losing 70% of site visitors - no retargeting strategy"
   
3. **Budget efficiency vs. scale** - Can you scale profitably?
   - Alert: "ROAS 5x at $10K/mo but drops to 2x at $30K/mo - audience size constraint"
   
4. **Lifetime value alignment** - Targeting wrong customers
   - Alert: "Acquiring $500 LTV customers for $400 - barely profitable, need better targeting"

## üìÑ PAGES (Landing Pages / Product Pages)

### ‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)
1. **Conversion rate crash** - 8% ‚Üí 0.5% overnight
   - Alert: "Checkout page broken - losing $X,XXX/day"
   
2. **Form abandonment spike** - 50% filling form, 2% submitting
   - Alert: "Form validation error or submission bug"
   
3. **Page load timeout** - 5+ second load time (was 2 seconds)
   - Alert: "Page performance degraded - users bouncing"
   
4. **Traffic spike with low conversions** - 10x traffic, same conversions
   - Alert: "Low-quality traffic source or page mismatch"

### üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)
1. **Conversion rate erosion** - Slowly declining over 3 weeks
   - Alert: "Landing page CVR down 15% - creative fatigue or message-market mismatch"
   
2. **Mobile vs. desktop gap** - Mobile CVR half of desktop
   - Alert: "Mobile experience causing 50% conversion loss - UX issue"
   
3. **Page element performance** - A/B test showing clear winner
   - Alert: "New CTA copy converting 25% better - implement across site"
   
4. **Scroll depth declining** - Users not reading full page
   - Alert: "Average scroll depth down to 40% - above-fold content needs improvement"

### üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. **Optimization gap** - No A/B testing in 6 months
   - Alert: "No optimization testing - leaving 20-30% conversions on table"
   
2. **High traffic, low conversion pages** - 10K visits, 0.5% CVR
   - Alert: "5 high-traffic pages converting poorly - $XX,XXX/mo opportunity"
   
3. **Page speed across site** - Average 4+ seconds
   - Alert: "Site-wide performance issues - losing 50% of mobile traffic"
   
4. **Trust signal gap** - No reviews, testimonials, or social proof
   - Alert: "Zero trust signals on site - adding social proof typically lifts CVR 15-30%"

## üö¶ TRAFFIC (Acquisition / Sources)

### ‚ö° Fast Detection (Daily)
1. **Traffic source disappears** - Organic down 80% suddenly
   - Alert: "Google penalty or technical issue - urgent investigation"
   
2. **Referral traffic spike** - Unexpected traffic from new source
   - Alert: "Featured on SiteX - 10K visitors today, capture with lead magnet"
   
3. **Direct traffic drop** - Email or brand searches down
   - Alert: "Direct traffic down 60% - email deliverability or brand issue?"
   
4. **Geographic anomaly** - Traffic from unexpected country
   - Alert: "5K visitors from [Country] - bot attack or viral international content?"

### üìà Trend Detection (Weekly)
1. **Channel shift** - Organic growing, paid declining
   - Alert: "SEO investments paying off - organic up 20% for 4 weeks"
   
2. **Quality by source trending** - Facebook traffic converting better
   - Alert: "Facebook CVR up 30% over 3 weeks - scale spend"
   
3. **Seasonality emerging** - Traffic patterns showing weekly rhythm
   - Alert: "Weekends converting 2x better - adjust ad scheduling"
   
4. **New traffic source emerging** - Reddit/HackerNews growing
   - Alert: "Reddit driving increasing traffic - community engagement opportunity"

### üó∫Ô∏è Strategic Overview (Monthly/Quarterly)
1. **Channel dependency risk** - 80% traffic from one source
   - Alert: "Over-reliant on Google - algorithm change = business risk"
   
2. **Attribution model weakness** - Last-click only
   - Alert: "No multi-touch attribution - under-valuing email, organic, social"
   
3. **Traffic quality analysis** - Which sources drive LTV?
   - Alert: "Organic traffic has 3x higher LTV than paid - shift investment"
   
4. **Acquisition cost trends** - CAC rising faster than LTV
   - Alert: "Unit economics deteriorating - CAC up 40%, LTV flat"
