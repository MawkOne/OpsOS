## ðŸ’¡ KEY INSIGHTS FROM RESEARCH

### 1. Speed Matters
- Industry standard: Detect ranking issues within **18 hours** (we're at ~weekly)
- Example: $4,200/month saved by catching issues 5 days earlier
- **Action:** Add daily monitoring layer

### 2. Gap Detection is Critical
- Most tools focus on "fix what's broken"
- Best practices emphasize "spot what's missing"
- Example: Email volume 50% below benchmark = missed revenue
- **Action:** Build benchmarking into detectors

- Our 3-layer framework (Fast/Trend/Strategic) aligns with industry
- But we're **heavy on Strategic, light on Fast**
- **Action:** Rebalance toward real-time alerts

### 4. Attribution is Table Stakes
- Multi-touch attribution considered essential for 2026
- Channel-specific ROI tracking expected
- **Action:** Add attribution detectors in Phase 2

### 5. AI/ML is the Norm

## ðŸŽ“ LEARNING: What Makes a Good Detector

Based on industry research, effective detectors should:

1. **Be Actionable** - Don't just say "traffic is down," say "organic traffic from blog posts down 40%, check indexing"
2. **Have Context** - Compare to baselines, benchmarks, and historical patterns
3. **Prevent, Not Just Detect** - Catch leading indicators (audience saturation) before lagging indicators (ROAS drops)
4. **Be Configurable** - Allow sensitivity tuning and custom thresholds
5. **Suggest Solutions** - Provide recommended actions, not just diagnosis
6. **Consider Confidence** - Use statistical significance and confidence intervals
7. **Distinguish Signal from Noise** - Don't alert on every fluctuation
